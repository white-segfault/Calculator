Calculator
Keyboard usage:
  • All buttons except for A/C have the same key.
  • For A/C button, it is replaced by Esc key.
Miscellaneous:
  • Floating numbers are at most rounded to 5 decimals places.
  • Invalid operations are not supported, i.e. divide by 0 or mod 0.
  • Largest number supported is 99999999999999 while smallest supported is 99999999999999
  • Top left for operations currently doing and bottom right for result or operand two
